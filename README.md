MaungRWA

Tokenize real-world assets (vehicles & invoices) into ERC-1155 NFTs with fractional ownership and on-chain yield distribution.

Live Demo → maung-rwa.vercel.app/

What it does

Tokenization — Real-world assets (rental vehicles, trade invoices) minted as ERC-1155 NFTs
Fractional ownership — Users buy fractions of an asset starting from 0.01 POL
Yield distribution — Owner distributes rental/invoice income on-chain, holders claim proportionally
Portfolio tracking — View held fractions and pending yield per wallet
